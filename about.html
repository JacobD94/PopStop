About PopStop

PopStop started as a colorful dream in a garage filled with fizz, flavor, and friends. Back in 2018, our founder, Jamie “Fizz” Ramirez, began experimenting with homemade soda recipes using fresh fruit, spices, and a wild imagination. What began as a neighborhood hit—served out of an ice chest at local events—quickly bubbled into a full-fledged passion. With a knack for unique flavors and a love for all things retro, Jamie transformed that fizz-fueled hobby into PopStop: an online soda shop where creativity and refreshment go hand in hand.

Today, PopStop celebrates the art of soda with bold branding, nostalgic vibes, and a rainbow of unforgettable flavors. We believe soda should be more than just a drink—it should be an experience. Whether you're craving a classic cola twist, a spicy mango fizz, or a seasonal limited edition, PopStop is your one-stop shop for bubbly happiness. Join us on our fizzy adventure and taste the fun in every pop!

What We Do

At PopStop, we craft and deliver small-batch sodas that are bursting with flavor and personality. Every bottle is made with real ingredients, imaginative recipes, and a splash of nostalgia. From inventing new fizzy favorites to shipping them straight to your doorstep, we’re here to make soda fun again—one pop at a time.
